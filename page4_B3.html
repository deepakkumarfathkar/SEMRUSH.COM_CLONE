Regular site audits are an integral part of maintaining a healthy website. However, a comprehensive site audit (particularly on a large website) isn’t easy. Every technical aspect of a site requires meticulous attention, creating a huge list of potential problems.

Frequently, the same mistakes and issues raise their heads time and time again. That’s why we analyzed 100,000 sites and 450 million pages to uncover the most common technical SEO issues found on websites.

Our Ultimate Site Audit with Semrush ebook explains how you can identify and then fix common technical SEO issues. It also offers guidance on auditing all sections of a site.

Within this Site Audit ebook you’ll find advice on:

  • Crawlability and site architecture: Robots.txt, URL structures, redirects, sitemaps
  • On-page SEO: Content, title tags, H1s, meta descriptions, images
  • Technical SEO: Page speed, old technology, mobile
  • HTTPS Implementation: Switching from HTTP to HTTPS
  • International SEO: Configuring a multilingual site, avoiding duplicates and redirects

Download this free guide today to cover all your bases when you perform your next site audit.

Estimated Reading Time: 20 minutes

Target Audience: This guide is built for mid-level SEOs at agencies or in-house who are regularly required to perform site audits.
